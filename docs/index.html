FAQ

Common questions

Everything you want to know before trusting us with your most personal information.

Does People Memory upload my data anywhere?

No. People Memory has zero network access. Every name, note, photo, and date is stored exclusively in your device's local private storage. The app does not contain any server communication, cloud sync, or analytics SDK.

What happens if I lose my phone?

Your data lives on your device. If you lose your phone, you can export a backup from within the app before switching devices. There is no automatic cloud backup by design — this is the privacy trade-off we make.

Is the app free?

Yes, completely free with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads. The code is open source on GitHub.

How are notifications sent without a server?

All reminders are local device notifications scheduled by your OS. When you set a meeting time or birthday reminder, your phone schedules it locally — no push notification server is involved.

Can I import contacts from my phone's address book?

Yes. People Memory can read your device contacts (with your permission) to help auto-fill names, phone numbers, and birthdays when adding a new person. Contact data is never synced back anywhere.

Is this a CRM (Customer Relationship Manager)?

It is designed as a personal CRM — focused on real human relationships, not sales pipelines. Think of it as an extended memory for your personal and professional life, not a business tool.

What platforms are supported?

People Memory is available on iOS (iPhone) and Android. It is built with React Native + Expo (SDK 54), targeting modern devices running iOS 16+ or Android 10+.
