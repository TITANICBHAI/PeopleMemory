⬇️ 1. Downloading the App

People Memory is available for free on Android via Uptodown. iOS support is coming soon.

1

Go to Uptodown

Visit the official listing page and tap Download.

https://people-memory.en.uptodown.com/android
2

Allow installation from unknown sources

On Android, go to Settings → Security → allow installs from your browser. This is normal for apps outside the Play Store.

3

Install and open

Tap the downloaded APK file and follow the prompts. The app installs in seconds.

✓ Safe to install: People Memory is open source. You can review every line of code at github.com/TITANICBHAI/PeopleMemory before installing.

🔐 2. First Launch & PIN Setup

The very first time you open People Memory, you'll be asked to create a 4-digit PIN. This PIN is the only thing protecting your data.

1

Choose your PIN

Pick any 4-digit number. Avoid obvious ones like 1234 or 0000. You will need this every time you open the app.

2

Confirm your PIN

Enter it again to confirm. Your PIN is immediately hashed (SHA-256) and stored only on your device. Nobody — including us — can ever read it.

3

Complete the tutorial

A one-time 4-step overlay walks you through the main features. Tap through it to reach the dashboard.

⚠️ Important: There is no "forgot PIN" option. If you forget your PIN, you will lose access to your data. Write it somewhere safe.

👤 3. Adding Your First Person

1

Tap the + button

On the dashboard, tap the blue + button in the bottom right corner.

2

Enter their name

The name is the only required field. Everything else is optional.

3

Set an avatar

Pick one of 12 preset emoji avatars, or tap the camera icon to use a photo from your device. If you skip this, the app shows their initials.

4

Fill in what you know

Add tags, a trust level, notes, birthday, and any other details. You can always edit these later — add things as you learn them.

5

Save

Tap Save. The person appears on your dashboard immediately.

📊 4. Understanding Trust Levels

Every person gets a trust score from 0 to 10. This is entirely personal and private — it never leaves your device.

What the colours mean

0

0–3 — Low Trust (Red)

Proceed carefully. You're still learning about this person, or past experiences have made you cautious.

5

4–6 — Moderate Trust (Yellow)

The relationship is building. You know them reasonably well but haven't fully established trust yet.

8

7–10 — High Trust (Green)

Your inner circle. People you rely on and can be fully open with.

Tip: Update trust levels over time as relationships evolve. A score of 5 today might become 8 in six months.

✨ 5. Using Key Features

Meeting Prep Cards

Before meeting someone, open their profile and tap Meeting Prep. You'll see a curated card with: what topics they care about, their upcoming dates, and things to remember. Walk in confident every time.

Voice Notes

After a meeting, open the person's profile and tap the microphone icon to record a voice note. It's automatically timestamped and saved to their interaction history on your device.

Relationship Health Score

Each person gets an auto-calculated health score based on: how recently you've interacted, upcoming meetings or birthdays, and how complete their profile is. A low score is a nudge to reach out.

Groups

Tap the Groups tab on the dashboard to create colour-coded groups (Work, Family, Friends, etc.) and assign people to them. View all members of a group at a glance.

Activity Journal

The Journal tab shows a single chronological feed of every interaction, birthday, and meeting across all your people — like a personal news feed of your relationships.

Search & Filter

Use the search bar on the dashboard to find anyone instantly. Use the tag filter row to show only people with a specific tag.

🔒 6. Your Privacy & Data

People Memory is built around a single principle: your data belongs to you.

  • All data is stored in your device's private local storage (AsyncStorage)
  • The app makes zero network requests — no internet connection is ever used
  • No analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting SDKs
  • Your PIN is SHA-256 hashed and never stored as plain text
  • Photos you add stay on your device and are never uploaded anywhere
Backup tip: Use the Export feature in app settings to save a local backup before switching phones. There is no automatic cloud backup by design.

🔍 7. Google Search Console Setup

This tells Google your site exists so it appears in search results. It's free and takes about 5 minutes.

1

Go to Google Search Console

Visit search.google.com/search-console and sign in with your Google account.

2

Add property → URL prefix

Click Add property. Choose URL prefix (not Domain). Enter exactly:

https://peoplememory.pages.dev/
3

Verify with HTML tag

Scroll down to the HTML tag method and click Verify. The tag is already on your site — it will confirm instantly.

4

Submit your sitemap

In the left menu, click Sitemaps → Add a new sitemap. Enter:

https://peoplememory.pages.dev/sitemap.xml
✓ What happens next: Google will crawl your site within a few days. You'll start seeing your pages in search results within 1–2 weeks. Check back in Search Console to see impressions and clicks grow.

🌐 8. Bing Webmaster Tools Setup

Bing powers search for Bing, DuckDuckGo, and several AI tools. Worth setting up — also free.

1

Go to Bing Webmaster Tools

Visit bing.com/webmasters and sign in with a Microsoft account.

2

Add your site

Click Add a site and enter:

https://peoplememory.pages.dev/
3

Verify with meta tag

Choose the HTML meta tag option and click Verify. The tag is already on your site.

4

Submit sitemap

Go to Sitemaps → Submit sitemap and enter:

https://peoplememory.pages.dev/sitemap.xml

📲 9. Updating Your Uptodown Listing

Your app is already on Uptodown. Here's how to update the description to include your website and improve discoverability.

1

Log into Uptodown Developer

Go to developer.uptodown.com and sign in to your developer account.

2

Find People Memory in your apps

Select People Memory from your dashboard.

3

Update the description

Copy and paste the text below into the description field:

People Memory is a 100% private personal relationship manager (PRM) for Android. Track trust levels, notes, birthdays, meetings, and interactions for every person you meet — without any data ever leaving your device. No cloud. No account. No tracking. Completely free. KEY FEATURES: • PIN-protected local vault (4-digit, SHA-256 hashed) • Trust level tracking (0–10 scale, red/yellow/green) • Rich notes — likes, dislikes, quick facts, descriptions • Dates & local reminders — birthdays, meetings, custom dates • Voice interaction notes with timestamps • Colour-coded groups (work, family, friends, networking) • Relationship health score (auto-calculated) • Meeting prep cards — walk into every meeting confident • Activity journal — chronological feed of all interactions PRIVACY FIRST: People Memory has zero network requests, zero analytics, zero advertising, and zero third-party SDKs. All your personal information stays on your device, forever. 🌐 Website: https://peoplememory.pages.dev 💻 Open source: https://github.com/TITANICBHAI/PeopleMemory
4

Add your website URL

In the Website field, enter:

https://peoplememory.pages.dev
5

Save and submit

Click Save. Uptodown will review changes and publish them within 24–48 hours.

✓ Why this matters: A detailed description with your website URL helps Uptodown rank your app higher in their search results, and lets AI tools and crawlers discover your official site from your app listing.
